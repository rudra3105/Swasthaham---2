Questions & Answers

Most common queries about the Swasthaham Diploma

The diploma is ideal for graduates and working professionals who want to build a career in wellness, as well as passionate learners looking to formalize their knowledge. No prior certification is required— a genuine interest in health, nutrition, and fitness is recommended.

The program typically runs 4–6 months depending on pace. It blends self-paced modules with live mentor sessions, assignments, and case studies. You’ll get structured timelines, weekly guidance, and access to learning resources and templates.

On completion, you’ll earn the Diploma in Holistic Wellness. You’ll also graduate with a portfolio of case studies, client-style plans, and assessment feedback—plus access to our alumni community for continued support and opportunities.

Click “Book a Counseling Call” to speak with our team. Enrollment includes all modules (nutrition, fitness, lifestyle, mental wellness), mentor sessions, case-based assignments, assessments with feedback, and community access. Fee details and upcoming cohorts are shared during your call.
